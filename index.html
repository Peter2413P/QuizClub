About Us

Welcome to the CIT Quiz Club, where knowledge meets excitement! We are a vibrant community of quiz enthusiasts at Coimbatore Institute of Technology, dedicated to fostering intellectual curiosity and competitive spirit.

Our club brings together students from all departments who share a passion for learning and challenging themselves across various domains of knowledge. From science and technology to history, literature, sports, and current affairs - we explore it all!

Join us for thrilling quiz competitions, engaging trivia nights, and knowledge-sharing sessions that will expand your horizons and connect you with like-minded individuals.

Community Guidelines

Respect: Treat all members with kindness and respect. We celebrate diversity and welcome students from all backgrounds.

Learning: Share knowledge freely and help fellow members grow. Everyone starts somewhere, and we support each other's journey.

Participation: Actively engage in discussions, events, and activities. Your involvement makes our community stronger.

Fun: While we take quizzing seriously, we believe learning should be enjoyable. Bring your enthusiasm and positive energy!
